Or as we call it, "A Dog is an animal but not every animal is a dog"

In this article we'll explain that :
* You can assign a Dog to a field of type Animal
```csdiff
+Animal a = new Dog();
```
* You cannot assign an Animal to a field of type Dog
```csdiff
-Dog a = new Animal();
```
